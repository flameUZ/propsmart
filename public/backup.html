Investment Tips

Tip 1: A net rental yield below 3% is considered weak. Aim for 5% or above for a solid investment.
Tip 2: Always add renovation costs to property price for the TRUE cost of a resale purchase.
Tip 3: Look beyond price — upcoming infrastructure like metro lines, highways, and schools significantly impact future value.
Tip 4: Your home loan EMI should not exceed 40% of your monthly income to maintain financial health.
Tip 5: Kitchen and bathroom renovations can boost property value by 10–15%. Living room updates add 5–8%.
Tip 6: Only purchase RERA-registered properties. This ensures legal protection and better resale value.
Warning: Be cautious of anyone promising "guaranteed returns" in real estate. No investment is without risk.
